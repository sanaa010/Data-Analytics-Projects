# Customer-Shopping-Behavior-Analysis

A complete end-to-end data analytics project focused on understanding customer purchasing behavior, identifying business trends, and generating actionable insights using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**.

This project analyzes customer shopping patterns across demographics, product categories, payment methods, discounts, subscriptions, and shipping preferences to help businesses improve customer engagement and optimize marketing strategies.

---

## 📌 Business Problem

A retail company wants to understand:

* What factors influence customer purchasing behavior?
* Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
* How discounts, subscriptions, shipping, and reviews affect sales?
* How to improve customer loyalty and retention?

The goal is to transform raw shopping data into meaningful business insights and interactive dashboards for data-driven decision-making.

## 📈 Key Insights

### 👩‍💼 Revenue by Gender

* Female customers generated slightly higher revenue than male customers.
* Gender-focused marketing campaigns can improve targeting efficiency.

### 💳 Subscription Impact

* Subscription customers spend significantly more than non-subscribers.
* Subscribers contribute a large share of total revenue.

### 🚚 Shipping Preferences

* Customers choosing express shipping spend more per transaction.
* Faster delivery services positively influence purchase value.

### 🏷️ Discount Behavior

* High-value customers actively use discounts.
* Personalized offers can improve customer retention and sales.

### ⭐ Product Satisfaction

* Certain product categories consistently receive higher ratings.
* Top-rated products can be promoted in marketing campaigns.
